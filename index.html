today we are gonna be talking about my 3 of my most favourites games!

Far Cry 5:

This game is one of my all-time favorites, partly due to the emotional connection I had while playing. The visuals are stunning, and although the story is just okay, the soundtrack, visuals, lighting, and location truly captivated me.

Dying Light:

I don't usually play zombie games, but this one was surprisingly good. The co-op support made it enjoyable with a friend, and the story, especially with the DLC, was excellent. It's now in my top 3 games.

Sleeping Dogs:

now many may think its another copy of gta 5. in my opinion it was much more than that. the hand to hand martial art really felt fun to play. also the lighting was very good. story was definitely on the higher end. overall really enjoyed the game!
